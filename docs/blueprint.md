# **App Name**: ABZ Automations

## Core Features:

- Static Content Display: Showcase company information, 'About Us', 'Stats', 'Products', 'Services', and 'Team' sections as described in the provided content. Ensure all text and images are presented clearly.
- Product & Service Details: Dedicated pages or detailed sections for flagship products like AutoX and various services (Water Pump Installation, Tank Cleaning, etc.), including 'Read More' or 'Learn More' links where applicable.
- Get a Quote Form: A user-friendly form allowing visitors to request quotes for products and services. Collect essential contact information and service interests.
- AI Quote Request Assistant: A generative AI tool that guides users through crafting a detailed quote request by asking clarifying questions about their needs and requirements for water automation solutions, improving accuracy and reducing back-and-forth.
- Interactive Location Map: Integrate an embedded Google Map pointing to the Kumasi Business Incubator location along with display of Ghana Post GPS coordinates.
- Social Media & Contact Info: Prominent display of contact details (phone, email) and clickable links to LinkedIn, X, Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram social media profiles.
- Responsive Design: The entire website must be fully responsive, providing an optimal viewing and interaction experience across all devices (desktops, tablets, and mobile phones).

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme to convey clarity and modernity. Primary brand color: a strong, technological blue (#1F72AD) reflecting automation and water, suitable for prominent headings and interactive elements. Background color: a subtle, almost white shade of the primary hue (#F0F3F5) to ensure readability and spaciousness. Accent color: a fresh, vibrant green (#2EAB5C) for calls-to-action and highlights, symbolizing growth and environmental consciousness.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-inspired, and impactful appearance. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, highly legible body text that maintains a professional and objective feel across all content.
- Utilize clean, modern, line-based, or geometric icons for navigation, features, and social media. Icons should align with the technical and professional nature of the business.
- Employ a spacious and minimalist layout with clear content hierarchy. Use ample white space to improve readability and draw attention to key information. Sections should be well-defined with consistent padding and alignment, promoting a clean, structured user experience.
- Incorporate smooth and elegant animation transitions for page loads and section reveals, gentle hover effects on interactive elements, and fluid scrolling effects for product cards and carousels. Animations should be functional, enhancing user experience with a premium feel, without being distracting.
